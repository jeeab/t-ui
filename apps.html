Installing an app

📲 The easy way — straight from the T‑Deck

  1. Make sure Wi‑Fi is set up once in Settings → Wi‑Fi.
  2. Tap Get Apps on the home screen — it turns Wi‑Fi on by itself and lists everything you see below.
  3. Tap Install. The app lands on your home screen right away — no restart, no computer, no card reader.

Needs firmware 2026.07.18.2 or newer — grab it from the installer. Apps already on your card show as “On device”.

or

💻 From a computer

  1. Download an app below — it's a tiny zip with one folder inside.
  2. Get to your T‑Deck's SD card: pop it into the computer, or use Settings → Share files on the T‑Deck (connect with FileZilla — address on the screen, user tdeck, password 1234).
  3. Unzip, and put the app's folder inside the card's apps folder (so you end up with e.g. apps/dice/main.lua).
  4. Restart the T‑Deck — the app appears on the home screen. To uninstall, delete its folder.

Make your own — and send it in

Apps are single text files written in a simple language called Lua — no programming tools needed, and you can hand the guide to an AI assistant and describe what you want.

📖 The app‑maker's guide  ·  📮 How to submit your app

Anyone can submit. Every submission is checked automatically for safety and size, then reviewed by hand before it goes in.

Apps run inside T‑UI's sandboxed app engine — they can draw, beep and read the touchscreen, nothing else. Part of the T‑UI project for the LilyGo T‑Deck.
